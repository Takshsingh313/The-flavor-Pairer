# The-flavor-Pairer
This is a flavor pairer in which you enter your flavor type and then your ingredient type and you get the dish duos of the specific ingredient. 
## Features
- Personalized Flavor Profile: Easily select and save your primary taste preference(Sweet, Savory, Spicy, Umami, Tart, or Bitter).
- Targeted Search Results: Search for any main ingredient and instantly receive dish instantly.
- Ingredient Discovery: If your search finds no matches, this app provides suggestions of available ingredients, prominently marking those that align with your personalized flavor profile with a star profile.
## Technologies 
- Front-End: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS.
- Firebase Backend: Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore.

## How to Use
- Open the link in your browser
- Enter your flavor Profile
- After that enter your ingredient
- a profile matching will appear with your profile
- That's it and your dish duos are on the screen.
